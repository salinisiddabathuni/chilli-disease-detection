DATASET INFORMATION
====================

Dataset used: PlantVillage Dataset
Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/emmarex/plantdisease

Classes used (8):
1. anthracnose       - 600 images (from Tomato Target Spot folder)
2. bacterial_spot    - 600 images (from Pepper Bell Bacterial Spot)
3. cercospora_spot   - 600 images (from Tomato Septoria Leaf Spot)
4. healthy           - 600 images (from Pepper Bell Healthy + Tomato Healthy)
5. leaf_curl         - 600 images (from Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl)
6. mosaic_virus      - 600 images (from Tomato Mosaic Virus)
7. powdery_mildew    - 600 images (from Tomato Leaf Mold)
8. thrips_damage     - 600 images (15 real + 485 augmented)

Total: 4,800 images (600 per class - balanced)

Augmentation used: rotation, horizontal flip, vertical flip,
zoom, brightness change, shear

Training: Google Colab (free GPU)
Model: MobileNetV2 alpha=0.35
Final accuracy: 87.4% validation accuracy
